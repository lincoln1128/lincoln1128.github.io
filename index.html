Ke Lin

Ke Lin is currently the Head of Algorithm Research at JD Technology, leading research on 2D digital humans, 3D digital humans, and image/video generation. Prior to this, he worked at Kuaishou Kling, Huawei, and Samsung. He received his Ph.D. from Tsinghua University, his bachelor's degree from Nanjing University, and completed postdoctoral research at Peking University.

  • 2021 – 2024: Huawei, Researcher (AIGC / AutoML)
