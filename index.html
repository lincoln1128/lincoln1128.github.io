About Me

Ke Lin is currently a principal researcher at JD Technology, leading the research on 2D digital humans, 3D digital humans, and image/video generation. Prior to this, he worked at Kuaishou Kling team, Huawei, and Samsung. He received his Ph.D. degree from Tsinghua University, adviced by Pro. Xiaorong Gao, and he received his bachelor's degree from Nanjing University. He completed his postdoctoral research at Peking University, advised by Pro. Liwei Wang. He has published over 20 papers in top-tier journals and conferences, including CVPR, AAAI, TIP and EMNLP.

We are actively looking for research interns and full-time researchers to work on AIGC, image generation, video generation. Please reach out to me at lincoln1128@gmail.com if you are interested.
