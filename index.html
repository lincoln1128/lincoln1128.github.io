Ke Lin.

AI Researcher & Engineer

linke.jason@jd.com

Ke Lin is currently an AI algorithm engineer at JD.com, working on multimodal AI, video understanding, and large language models. He received his M.S. from the State Key Laboratory of Intelligent Technologies, Tsinghua University, where he was advised by Prof. Shangkai Gao and Prof. Xiaorong Gao, working on brain-computer interfaces (BCI) and neural signal processing.

His research spans multimodal learning, video captioning, vision-language models, and brain-computer interfaces. He has published in top venues including AAAI, EMNLP, IEEE TIP, IEEE TNNLS, Journal of Neural Engineering, and CVPR. His work has received over 500+ citations on Google Scholar.
